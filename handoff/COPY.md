# Voice, Tone, Microcopy

TBLStats isn't a sportsbook and it isn't ESPN. Think **The Ring Magazine meets a modern stats desk**. Plain-spoken, a little literary, respects the reader.

## Voice principles

1. **Name the thing.** Say "welterweight," not "the 147-pound division." Say "decision," not "non-KO outcome." Boxing has a vocabulary; use it.
2. **Let numbers carry the weight.** Don't decorate a +10 with "impressive" or "incredible." The number is the sentence.
3. **Editorial beats product-y.** "Call the card" beats "Submit your picks." "The Ledger" beats "Stats Dashboard."
4. **Never hype.** No fire emoji, no all-caps excitement, no "🔥 STREAK." A W4 streak speaks for itself.
5. **Second-person sparingly.** "You" is fine in the pick'em flow. Everywhere else, stay third-person.

## Dos and don'ts

| ✅ Say | ❌ Don't say |
|---|---|
| Call the card. | Make your picks! |
| Thirteen of sixteen rounds. | 81% win rate on rounds |
| Top of the net-points table. | #1 in the rankings 🥇 |
| Locks at first bell. | Picks close Friday night |
| Every round. Every fighter. Every team. | Your #1 source for boxing stats |
| Decision — 4 rounds | W by UD |
| The sharpest eyes in boxing. | Top predictors |

## Microcopy library

### Empty states
- Pick'em, no picks yet: **"Four bouts this weekend. Pick them all."**
- Leaderboard, no entries yet: **"First bell hasn't rung. Nobody's on the board."**
- Fighter's fight log, rookie: **"No fights on the card yet. Check back Friday."**
- Standings pre-season: **"Week 1 opens [date]. The ledger is blank."**

### Action buttons

| Context | Copy |
|---|---|
| Submit picks | **Lock picks →** |
| Save fighter follow | **Follow** |
| See full list | **See all [X] →** |
| Share | **Copy link** |
| Next page | **Next week →** |

### Confirmation/success

- Picks submitted: **"Picks locked. See you at first bell."**
- Account created: **"Welcome to the ledger."**
- Unfollow: **"Unfollowed. You can always come back."**

### Errors

Polite, brief, specific.

- Network fail: **"Can't reach the ledger. Try again in a moment."**
- Validation — pick missing: **"Two bouts still open. Finish the card."**
- Lock-expired: **"Card's locked. Picks are final."**

### Kickers/eyebrows

These go above section titles and page masts. Short, uppercase, letter-spaced.

- WEEK 05 · SEASON 2026
- THE WAGER · WEEK 5
- THE BOARD · SEASON 2026
- FIGHTER PROFILE · VOL. II
- FRIDAY NIGHT · MAIN CARD

### Tagline candidates

Use the first as the primary brand tagline; the others for specific surfaces.

1. **Every round. Every fighter. Every team.** (primary)
2. **The independent ledger of the Team Boxing League.**
3. **Where the numbers get their say.**

## Date/time formats

- Event time: **Fri · 8:00 PM ET**
- Date in body copy: **Apr 24, 2026**
- Timestamp in logs: **2026-04-24 20:00 ET**
- Countdown: **HH:MM:SS** (mono font, red at < 1 hr)
- Week reference: **Wk 05** (lists/badges) · **Week 5** (prose)

## Numbers

- Always show the sign on differentials: **+10.0**, **−3.0** (use a real minus, not a hyphen)
- Always one decimal for net points: **+10.0** not **+10**
- Integers for rounds, picks, points
- Commas on numbers >= 10,000
- Record format: **4-0** (hyphen, no spaces); with draws: **4-0-1**

## Abbreviations

Spell out on first mention in long-form copy. In tables/badges, these are the approved abbreviations:

- **W/L/D** — win/loss/draw
- **W4** / **L2** — streak
- **NPPR** — net points per round (always all-caps)
- **WAR** — wins above replacement
- **Diff** — point differential
- **PF/PA** — points for / against
- **Wk** — week
- Division: **FLY · BW · FW · LW · WW · MW · LHW · HW**
- Stance: **ORT / SO / SW** (avoid in prose; say orthodox/southpaw/switch)
